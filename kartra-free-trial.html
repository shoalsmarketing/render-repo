About the Platform

Built by internet marketing veterans Andy Jenkins and Mike Filsaime, the platform emerged from practitioner insight rather than theoretical product development. Years of running digital businesses informed feature prioritization and user experience design. This origin influences everything from workflow architecture to capability depth.

The core proposition addresses tool fragmentation. Instead of managing separate subscriptions for email, pages, checkouts, memberships, scheduling, support, and affiliates—each requiring integration—everything operates within unified infrastructure. This consolidation promises cost reduction and operational simplification.

Target audiences include digital entrepreneurs, course creators, coaches, consultants, and marketing agencies. E-commerce operations with physical inventory might find specialized platforms better suited to fulfillment complexity. Understanding positioning helps evaluate fit against specific business models.

Core Capabilities

Page Building

The visual editor enables creating professional pages without coding. Component libraries provide pre-designed elements arranging through drag-and-drop. Templates cover common scenarios—squeeze pages, sales letters, webinar registrations. Responsive design automation ensures proper display across devices.

Email Marketing

Beyond broadcasts, the email system excels at behavioral automation. Sequences trigger based on subscriber actions. Conditional logic creates personalized experiences at scale. Segmentation organizes subscribers by behavior, source, or purchase history. Deliverability infrastructure maintains sender reputation.

Sales Systems

Payment processing connects through Stripe, PayPal, and other processors. Subscription billing handles recurring charges with automated dunning. One-click upsells and order bumps increase transaction values without complex configuration.

Additional Tools

Membership site functionality delivers digital products natively. Helpdesk ticketing handles support requests. Calendar scheduling manages appointments. Affiliate management enables partner programs. Each supplementary tool eliminates external platform dependencies.

Integration Advantage: When all functions operate within unified systems, automation possibilities expand dramatically. Triggers and actions requiring complex integration elsewhere happen automatically here.
